Who We Are

PahiloPaila - The First Step

"PahiloPaila" means "First Step" in Nepali, and that's exactly what we are—your gateway to experiencing Nepal beyond the guidebooks. We're a passionate team of local Nepalis and travel enthusiasts who believe the real magic happens when you step off the beaten path.

Founded in the heart of Kathmandu by tech innovators and cultural ambassadors, we've made it our mission to bridge the gap between curious travelers and the incredible local communities waiting to share their world.

Our Mission

Tourism That Gives Back

We believe travel should create meaningful impact. Every journey booked through PahiloPaila directly supports rural families, preserves traditional cultures, and creates sustainable opportunities in communities that need it most.

Our guides aren't just showing you places—they're sharing their homes, their stories, and their heritage. You're not just a tourist; you become part of their extended family, creating bonds that last far beyond your visit.
