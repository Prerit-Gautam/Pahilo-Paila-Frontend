About PahiloPaila

Nepal's beauty attracts the world — but the locals who bring it to life often stay unseen. PahiloPaila connects travelers with local guides, hosts, and rural communities through AI-driven cultural matching.

We go beyond booking trips — we match people, stories, and experiences. Tourists find authentic stays and guides that fit their mood and interests, while locals gain real visibility, income, and pride.

With PahiloPaila, every journey becomes the first step toward empowering Nepal — one connection at a time.
