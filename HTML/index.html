About PahiloPaila

PahiloPaila means "First Step" in Nepali, and we're here to guide your first steps into the authentic rural Nepal experience.

Our mission is to promote sustainable tourism in Nepal's rural areas, preserving local cultures while providing unforgettable experiences for travelers.

Founded in 2018 by a group of passionate local guides from different regions of Nepal, PahiloPaila aims to showcase the hidden beauty of rural Nepal that often goes unnoticed by mainstream tourism.

We work directly with local communities to ensure that tourism benefits the people who call these beautiful places home. By choosing PahiloPaila, you're supporting responsible tourism that respects both the environment and cultural heritage of Nepal.
